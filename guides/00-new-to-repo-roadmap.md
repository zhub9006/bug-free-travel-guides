## 🚨 Critical Corrections

| ❌ Myth | ✅ Reality |
|---|---|
| "Seychelles is bug-free" | Seychelles has mosquitoes year-round; peak Dec–Feb (rainy season) |
| "Iceland has zero mosquitoes" | *Culiseta annulata* found Oct 2025 — still likely safe, but verify |
| "Patagonia is always safe" | **ONLY** Jun–Aug! Nov–Mar = sandfly hell |
| "Hawaii is mosquito-free" | Kona coast is 2/10; Hilo side is 5+/10; peaks Sept–Nov |
| "Maldives is bug-free" | Only resort islands are safe; local islands have mosquitoes |
| "The Maldives is a safe bug-free destination" | Only resort islands have pest control; local islands = high bug risk |
| "There are only 2 mosquito-free places" | Antarctica and Iceland were historically the only ones; now we have 14+ |

## 💡 Quick Tips for Bug-Phobic Travelers

- **Pack DEET 30%+** as insurance — even at 0/10 destinations
- **Check mosquito reports** before booking — Google "[destination] mosquitoes 2026"
- **Climate change is real** — mosquitoes are expanding range at 0.2°C/decade warming
- **Always ask about resort pest control** for hotel/std bookings
- **Read the Welcome Guide FIRST** for critical 2025–2026 corrections

## 🎯 What Makes a Destination Bug-Free?

**7 Natural Mechanisms:**

| Mechanism | Where Applied | Science |
|---|---|---|
| **Island Isolation** | Faroe Islands, Montserrat, Curaçao, Maldives | Mosquitoes can't fly across oceans |
| **Extreme Cold** | Antarctica, Hokkaido (winter), Svalbard | Sub-zero kills all larvae/cold prevents breeding |
| **Extreme Aridity** | Atacama Desert, Dubai/Oman, Sedona, Curaçao | No standing water = no breeding habitat |
| **High Altitude** | Cusco, Puebla, Sedona, Atacama | Thin air, UV radiation, cool temps suppress breeding |
| **Constant Wind** | Curaçao, Madeira, Faroe Islands, Hawaii Kona | Mosquitoes are weak fliers; wind disrupts flight |
| **Acidic Soil/Water** | Hawaii, volcanic islands | Acidic water (pH<5) kills mosquito larvae |
| **Cool Rainy Climate** | London, Pacific NW, UK/EU cities | Cool temps prevent water from warming enough for breeding |
