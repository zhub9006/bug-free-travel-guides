## 🌍 Regional Quick Picks

### 🏝️ Caribbean (Only Safe Option!)
- **Montserrat** ⭐ (0.5/10) — Dec–Apr. Volcanic soil, mountain terrain, zero standing water.
- **Curaçao** ⭐ (2/10) — Dec–Apr. Hyper-arid, trade winds, outside hurricane belt.
- ❌ **Avoid:** Jamaica, Dominican Republic, Barbados, Puerto Rico (high mosquitoes!).

### 🏜️ Desert Safaris
- **Atacama, Chile** (0/10) — Feb–May or Sep–Nov. Driest on Earth.
- **UAE / Oman** (1/10) — Oct–Apr. Extreme heat, no standing water.
- **Sedona, AZ** (0/10) — Mar–May / Sep–Nov. Red rock, arid, zero bugs.

### 🏔️ Mountains & Cold Climates
- **Faroe Islands** (0/10) — May–Sep. Wind fortress.
- **Hokkaido, Japan (Winter)** (0/10) — Dec–Mar. Snow kills all larvae.
- **Patagonia Winter** (0/10) — **JUN–AUG ONLY.** Nov–Mar = sandfly hell!
- **Northern Norway (Lofoten)** (0–1/10) — May–Jun or Sep–Oct.
- **Scotland Highlands** (0/10) — May–Jun or Sep–Oct.

### 🌺 Tropical (Managed, Not Zero)
- **New Caledonia** (1.5/10) — May–Oct. Dry season = nearly bug-free.
- **Maldives Resorts** (2/10) — Dec–Apr. Resort pest control only!
- **Hawaii (Kona Coast)** (2/10) — Apr–Oct. Stay on west side!
- **French Polynesia** (3/10) — May–Oct. Avoid wet season (Nov–Apr).
- ❌ **Avoid:** Maldives local islands in wet season, Seychelles, Bali, Thailand.

### 🏙️ Urban City Breaks (Zero-Bug Cities)
- **London** (0/10) — Any month. Rain = zero bugs.
- **Paris** (0/10) — Any month. Cool, rainy, concrete jungle.
- **Amsterdam** (0/10) — Any month. Same marine climate effect.
- **Rome, Barcelona, Copenhagen, Vienna, Lisbon** — All 0/10 year-round.
- **Tokyo** — 0/10 in winter; slight risk in summer but manageable.
- ❌ **Avoid:** Bali, Bangkok, Jakarta — extremely high mosquitoes.

### 🏔️ US National Parks
- **Big Bend Ranch, TX** (0/10) — Oct–Apr. One of the remotest places in the US.
- **Desert Southwest (Sedona, Las Vegas)** (0/10) — Oct–Apr. Arid perfection.
- **Glacier NP, MT** (1/10) — Jun–Sep. High altitude, cold nights.
- **Zion NP, UT** (1/10) — Mar–May / Sep–Nov. Dry canyon.
- **Kailua-Kona, HI** (2/10) — Apr–Oct. Stay on west side, never Hilo!

## ⚠️ THE SEASONAL TRAP WARNING

| Destination | Safe Window | DANGER Window | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Patagonia** | Jun–Aug (their winter!) 0/10 | Nov–Mar (their summer) 10/10 sandfly hell | **JUN–AUG ONLY** |
| **All Caribbean** | Dec–Apr dry season | Jul–Nov (wet + hurricane) | **Montserrat & Curaçao are exceptions** |
| **All Tropics** | Dry season months | Wet season = mosquito explosion | **Check the season FIRST** |
| **All deserts** | Oct–Apr (cooler months) | May–Sep (extreme heat + possible brief rain) | **Still mostly safe** |

## 🎯 5-Step Decision Tree: Where Should I Go?

1. **What's your budget?**
   - 💰 Under $50/day → Big Bend Ranch, Atacama, Sedona
   - 💰💰 $50–$150/day → Curaçao, Madeira, Hokkaido
   - 💰💰💰 $150–$500/day → Maldives resorts, Hawaii, French Polynesia
   - 💰💰💰💰 $500+/day → Antarctica, New Caledonia

2. **What's your travel style?**
   - 🏖️ Beach → Montserrat ⭐, Curaçao ⭐, Maldives resorts
   - 🏕️ Hiking/Adventure → Atacama, Faroe Islands, Patagonia winter
   - 🏙️ City break → UK/EU cities (any!), Tokyo
   - 🌵 Desert → Atacama, UAE/Oman, Sedona, Big Bend

3. **What's your tolerance level?**
   - 🛑 Severe phobia → Antarctica, Faroe Islands, Montserrat, Hokkaido, Atacama
   - ⚠️ Mild aversion → New Caledonia, Hawaii Kona, Maldives resorts, Curaçao
   - 🌡️ Can handle DEET → French Polynesia, Patagonia winter, UAE, Hawaii

4. **When are you traveling?**
   - 🌨️ Winter (Dec–Feb) → Antarctica, Madeira, Hokkaido, Atacama, Montserrat, Curaçao
   - 🌸 Spring (Mar–May) → Atacama, New Caledonia, Madeira, Curaçao
   - ☀️ Summer (Jun–Aug) → Faroe Islands, Iceland (verify!), Patagonia Win!, New Caledonia
   - 🍂 Autumn (Sep–Nov) → Atacama, New Caledonia, UAE/Oman, Montserrat, Curaçao

5. **How far are you willing to fly?**
   - 🏠 US only → Atacama, Curaçao, Hawaii Kona, Big Bend, Sedona
   - 🌍 Europe → Iceland (verify!), Faroe Islands, Madeira, UK/EU cities, Atacama
   - ✈️ Asia → Hokkaido, New Caledonia, Maldives resorts
   - 🧊 Polar → Antarctica (from South America or NZ), Arctic Norway, Svalbard

## 🚫 Destinations to ABSOLUTELY AVOID (for severe entomophobia)

| ❌ Destination | Why | Bug Score |
|---|---|---|
| **Seychelles** | Mosquitoes year-round; peak Dec–Feb. Commonly mislabeled as bug-free! | 8/10 |
| **Bali, Indonesia** | Extreme mosquito density; dengue everywhere | 9/10 |
| **Most of Southeast Asia** (Thailand, Myanmar, Vietnam south) | Heavy mosquitoes year-round; malaria/dengue risk | 7–9/10 |
| **Amazon Basin** | Maximum mosquito density; malaria, yellow fever | 10/10 |
| **Most mainland Africa** | Heavy mosquito populations; malaria risk | 6–9/10 |
| **Costa Rica** (except highlands) | High mosquitoes; Caribbean coast worst | 6/10 |
| **Papua New Guinea** | Extreme malaria zones; worst mosquito density on Earth | 10/10 |
| **Belize** | Highest mosquito density in Central America | 8/10 |
| **Patagonia (Nov–Mar)** | Sandfly hell — thousands at dawn/dusk | 10/10 |
