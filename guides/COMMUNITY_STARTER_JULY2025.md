## ✍️ How to Share Your Travel Experience

### 🌍 Add a Destination (Easiest Way!)
Open a **GitHub Issue** with this template:

```
Title: New Destination: [Place Name]

📍 Location: [Country, specific city/area]
🐛 My experience: [Did you see any bugs? How bad was it?]
📅 Best months: [When are insects least active?]
❌ Worst months: [When should I avoid?]
🔬 Why it works: [Is it the wind? The cold? The dryness? Resort treatment?]
⚠️ Exceptions: [Any surprising bug encounters or caveats?]
💰 Budget range: [Approximate $$-$$$$]
🔗 Sources: [Links to travel blogs, tourism boards, pest reports]
```

### 📖 Rate & Review Existing Destinations
- Did a destination NOT match our research? Let us know!
- Found a better season or a new bug-free spot? Share it!

### 🚨 Seasonal Alerts
- Got real-time info about an insect outbreak? Report it!
- Got cancelled due to unexpected bugs? Tell us!

## 🧳 Universal Bug-Phobic Packing Checklist

- [ ] DEET or Picaridin repellent (20%+)
- [ ] Permethrin-treated clothing
- [ ] AC / screened accommodation
- [ ] Portable fan
- [ ] Higher-floor hotel room
- [ ] Seasonal weather layers
- [ ] Travel insurance that covers insect-borne illness

## 🤝 Community Guidelines

1. **Be honest** — if a destination had bugs, say so!
2. **Cite sources** — travel blogs, pest reports, tourism boards
3. **Respect fear levels** — what's fine for one person may be terrifying for another
4. **No judgment** — we're all here because bugs ruin trips for us
5. **Have fun** — we deserve vacation worry-free!
