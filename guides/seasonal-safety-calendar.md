## ⚠️ "DO NOT GO" Destinations and Seasons

| Timeframe | Region to AVOID | Why? | Safe Alternative Pick |
|---|---|---|---|
| All year | Amazon Basin (Brazil/Peru/Colombia) | Mosquitoes every minute; malaria, dengue, yellow fever endemic | Madeira / Atacama deep desert interior |
| May–Sep (May–Mar for Seychelles) | tropical West/Central Africa, Seychelles (rainy season) | Mosquitoes 24/7; severe malaria/dengue/chikungunya burden; Seychelles wet season has peak mosquito activity | Northern Africa highlands / Madeira dry season |
| All year | Madagascar southern lowlands | Malaria + dengue + filariasis | Madagascar highlands only (Antananarivo area) |
| All year | Caribbean tropical lowlands | Mosquitoes every season; chikungunya on some islands | Madeira, Canary Islands at altitude (cooler) |
| Nov–Mar (boreal summer) | Patagonia (Argentina/Chile) | Worst bug season on Earth for bug-sensitive travelers — sandflies swarm endlessly | Patagonia **JUN–AUG** (their winter) |
| May–Jul | Southern US (SE states) + Canadian boreal forests | Mosquito peak; ticks ramping up | Pacific Northwest (cooler, drier, fewer bugs) |
| Apr–Aug | Southeast Asia | Dengue zone especially Thai/KH coastal lowlands | Japan Hokkaido winter (Dec–March) |
| Dec–Feb | Seychelles (rainy season!) | Mosquitoes peak indoors and outdoors; high humidity enables breeding | Seychelles dry season **May–Oct** ONLY |

## 🔬 Key Notes

- **Iceland ⚠️**: *Culiseta annulata* mosquitoes discovered Oct 2025 — no longer 100% guaranteed. Still lowest-risk warm bug-free option, but pack DEET.
- **New Caledonia**: Best May–Oct (dry season). SE trade winds + volcanic soil + endemic plant repellents keep insects minimal on Grand Terre. Transition months (Apr/Nov) are acceptable; Dec–Mar wet season sees moderate insect uptick on outer islands.
- **Seychelles correction**: Not truly mosquito-free! Best window is May–October (dry season). Dec–February is peak mosquito season — AVOID if entomophobia is severe.
