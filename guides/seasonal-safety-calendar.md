## 🔥 PATAGONIA: TWO-TONED SEASONAL COIN

| Season | Months | Bug Level | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SUMMER (DANGER!)** | Nov – Mar | **10/10 sandfly hell** | 🚫 DO NOT VISIT if phobic |
| **WINTER (SAFE!)** | Jun – Aug | **0/10 bug-free** | ✅ PERFECT window |

> ⚠️ Patagonia is essentially TWO DIFFERENT DESTINATIONS depending on the month. Summer brings aggressive sandflies (*Culicoides*) that swarm in thousands. Winter is pristine, cold, and completely bug-free. **Only book for Jun–Aug.**

## 🌴 CARIBBEAN: THE MONTSSERRAT & CURAÇAO EXCEPTION

| Destination | Peak Dry Season | Wet Season Risk | Bug Score |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🇲🇸 Montserrat | Dec – Apr ⭐ | May – Nov | **0.5/10** |
| 🇨🇼 Curaçao | Dec – Apr ⭐ | May – Nov | **2/10** |

> This is the ONLY viable bug-free Caribbean destination. All other Caribbean islands have significant mosquito populations in wet season. Montserrat's volcanic soil and mountain terrain drain water and prevent breeding. Curaçao's hyper-arid climate + trade winds create natural bug barriers.

## 🧴 DEET Insurance Policy

Even in the safest bug-free destinations, always pack DEET as insurance:

| Risk Level | DEET Strength | When to Use |
|---|---|---|
| 0/10 ✅ | 20% or light spray | Dawn/dusk near water or gardens |
| 0.5–2/10 ⭐ | 20–30% DEET | Standard application at dusk |
| 3–4/10 | 30–50% DEET | Need mandatory application |
| 5+/10 | 50%+ DEET + permethrin clothing | Full protection protocol |

## 📡 Real-Time Monitoring Protocol

Before booking any trip, check these resources:
1. **CDC Travelers' Health** — `wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel` — Malaria & bug alerts
2. **WHO Disease Outbreak News** — `who.int/emergencies` — Real-time outbreak tracking
3. **Climate anomalies** — Check for unusual rainy seasons that may cause early mosquito hatching
4. **Local tourism boards** — Sometimes know current conditions better than global databases
