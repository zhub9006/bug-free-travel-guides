## 🌷 Spring (Mar–May)
- **Top picks:** Atacama (perfect!), Madeira, Curaçao, UAE/Oman
- **Emerging:** New Caledonia dry season starts mid-April
- **Avoid:** Seychelles wet, Patagonia meltdown (Mar–May)

## ☀️ Summer (Jun–Aug)
- **Top picks:** Faroe Islands, Iceland ⚠️verify, Madeira, New Caledonia, Maldives resorts
- **Emerging:** Hawaii Kona season (best April–Oct)
- **Avoid:** Patagonia (WORST time!), Africa, S.E. Asia

## 🍂 Autumn (Sep–Nov)
- **Top picks:** New Caledonia (peak dry), French Polynesia (early dry), Atacama autumn, Montserrat
- **Emerging:** Caribbean window opens mid-Nov (Curaçao, Montserrat)
- **Avoid:** Seychelles wetting up, French Polynesia late wet

## ❄️ Winter (Dec–Feb)
- **Top picks:** Antarctica (prime season!), Hokkaido winter, Madeira, Atacama, UK/EU Cities
- **Emerging:** Best Caribbean window (Montserrat & Curaçao)
- **Avoid:** Seychelles (peak mosquitoes), French Polynesia (wet season)

## 🔥 Critical Seasonal Traps

### Patagonia — The Bi-Annual Trap ⚠️
| Season | Safety | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Jun–Aug** | ✅ SAFE | Winter. Cold, dry, no bugs. Only window for phobics. |
| **Sep–Oct** | ⚠️ Transitional | Sandflies start appearing. Risky. |
| **Nov–Mar** | 🚫 DANGER | **Sandfly HELL.** Worst months. Do NOT visit if phobic. |
| **Apr–May** | ⚠️ Transitional | Still risky. Better than summer, but not safe. |

### Seychelles — The False Promise 🚫
| Season | Safety | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| **All year** | 🚫 UNSAFE | Mosquitoes present year-round, peak Dec–Feb rains. |
| **Dec–Feb** | 🚫 WORST | Peak rainy season = peak mosquitoes. |
| **Aug–Oct** | 🚫 Still bad | "Dry" season doesn't mean bug-free. Much better off in Montserrat or Curaçao. |

### French Polynesia — Seasonal Sweet Spot
| Season | Safety | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| **May–Oct** | ✅ Manageable (3/10) | Dry season. Best window. Pack DEET. |
| **Nov–Apr** | 🚫 Dangerous (6+/10) | Wet season. Mosquitoes everywhere. Avoid. |

### Caribbean — The Montserrat/Curaçao Window 🌿
| Season | Safety | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Dec–Apr** | ⭐ Best window (0.5–2/10) | Peak dry season. Only truly safe Caribbean time. |
| **May–Nov** | 🚫 Risky (5+/10) | Hurricane belt + rains = mosquitoes. Not worth it. |
