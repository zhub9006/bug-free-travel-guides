## 🔬 6 Environmental Science Mechanisms — Why These Places Are Bug-Free

| # | Mechanism | Destinations | Scientific Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Temperature Kill Zone** | Antarctica, Iceland, Faroe, Hokkaido, Svalbard | Temps below 10°C kill mosquito larvae; brief summer warmth insufficient for breeding cycle |
| 2 | **Aridity Barrier** | Atacama, Dubai/Oman, Phoenix, Curaçao | <100mm annual rainfall; no standing water = no breeding. Explore.com: *"Mosquitoes love water..."* |
| 3 | **Island Biogeography** | Madeira, Montserrat, Azores, Canary Islands, New Caledonia | Remote islands have fewer colonizing species; volcanic drainage amplifies effect |
| 4 | **Volcanic Drainage** | Montserrat, Madeira, Azores, Kona Hawaii | Porous volcanic stone absorbs water instantly; ash deposits chemically kill larvae |
| 5 | **Resort Pest Management** | Maldives, Disney World, The Brando | Active traps, larvicide, sentinel chickens, eco-friendly traps (ONLY on managed properties) |
| 6 | **Marine Climate** | UK, EU cities, Azores | Cool rainy air prevents breeding; wind + maritime moderation suppresses all insect activity |
