## 🤧 Why This Community Exists

You've probably heard "just go to Iceland!" or "Montserrat is beautiful!" — but then you discover hidden sandflies, unexpected mosquitoes, or seasonal traps you never knew about. **You deserve better than that.**

This community exists to:
- **Research thoroughly** — We verify bug-free claims with multiple sources and seasonal data
- **Warn about seasonal traps** — Tourist sites say "bug-free!" but mean "dry season, NOT rain season." We tell you the truth.
- **Share real experiences** — Members report actual bug sightings so future travelers can plan accurately
- **Build a safe map** — Together, we're creating the world's most comprehensive bug-free travel database

## 📏 Our Community Standards

### ✅ Do:
- Cite **at least 2 independent sources** for any destination claim
- Specify **exact seasonal windows** (not just "winter" — say "June–August")
- Include a **bug safety score (0–10)** with clear justification
- Flag **transitional/trap seasons** clearly (e.g., "Patagonia Nov–Mar = sandfly hell!")
- Report **your own actual experiences** (sightings, no-bug zones, etc.)

### ❌ Don't:
- **Don't say a place is "bug-free" without specifying the season** — Seasonal specificity is EVERYTHING
- **Don't rely on outdated info** — Climate change is shifting insect ranges; always verify within 12 months
- **Don't dismiss others' valid bug sightings** — If someone reports mosquitoes, take it seriously and update the guide
- **Don't spread fear about places that are genuinely safe** — We're about informed confidence, not misinformation

## 🔍 Quick Bug Score Reference

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **0/10** ✅ | Zero bugs. 100% safe. No DEET needed. | Perfect destination |
| **0.5/10** ⭐ | Practically zero. Closest to bug-free on Earth | Excellent — pack DEET as insurance |
| **1–2/10** | Very low. Rare mosquitoes in specific spots/seasons | Good — pack DEET & wear long sleeves at dawn/dusk |
| **3–4/10** | Low. Manageable with DEET and planning | Acceptable — stick to dry season, use resort pest control |
| **5+ /10** | Moderate–high. Active mosquito populations | ⚠️ NOT recommended for severe entomophobia |

## 🚨 Critical Warnings (Read Before You Book!)

- 🚨 **Iceland:** Mosquitoes discovered Oct 2025. **VERIFY before booking.** Cold-adapted species; breeding unconfirmed. Pack DEET.
- 🚨 **Seychelles:** **NOT bug-free.** Mosquitoes year-round, peak Dec–Feb. Much better off in Montserrat or Curaçao!
- 🚨 **Patagonia:** **June–August ONLY.** Nov–Mar = sandfly hell. DO NOT visit in summer if you're a bug-phobe.
- 🚨 **Antarctica:** NOW the only guaranteed 100% mosquito-free landmass (was Iceland before Oct 2025).
- 🚨 **French Polynesia:** Dry season (May–Oct) only. Wet season (Nov–Apr) = mosquitoes + dengue risk.
- 🚨 **Desertas Islands (Madeira):** Home to *Segestria saeva* spider. Avoid if phobic. Stay on Madeira main island.
- 🚨 **Kruger Lowveld (South Africa):** Malaria zone. Stick to Western Cape & Garden Route.
