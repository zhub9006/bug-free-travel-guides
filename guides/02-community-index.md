## 🤝 Community Principles

1. **Be kind.** We're all here because bugs make us anxious. Support each other — no judgment.
2. **Verify before you trust.** Even guides say "verify within 12 months." Climate change is real.
3. **Pack DEET as insurance.** Even zero-bug zones get surprises. A spare bottle in your bag is worth its weight in gold.
4. **Share generously.** Your knowledge could save a fellow traveler's vacation.
