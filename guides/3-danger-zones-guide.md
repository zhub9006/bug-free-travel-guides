## 🆘 Emergency: Already Booked a Dangerous Destination?

1. **Research the SPECIFIC zone** — Maybe only part is dangerous? (e.g., Hawaii: Kona OK, Hilo/Puna NOT)
2. **Book resort with proven pest control** — 2 pts safer could mean 4→2 (acceptable vs dangerous)
3. **Limit outdoor exposure** — Stay indoors 10am–3pm; mosquitoes most active at dawn/dusk
4. **Permethrin-treated clothing** — Kills on contact; lasts 6+ washes
5. **Bring a head net** — Wear at dawn/dusk; effective 100%
6. **AC ON at ALL times** — Never open windows in tropics
7. **DEET 30–50% + Picaridin combo** — Apply to ALL exposed skin every 4 hours
8. **Sleep under permethrin-treated net** — Even in "safe" hotels, bed nets save lives
9. **Anti-malarial medication** — Visit travel clinic before trip; take as prescribed
10. **Know nearest hospital** — Save medical facility addresses before arriving
11. **Bedside DEET** — Mosquitoes smell CO2 from your breath and are already heading for you!
12. **Report encounters** — Open a GitHub Issue with `bug-report`!

> 🚫 **The best defense against bugs isn't DEET — it's choosing the RIGHT destination in the RIGHT SEASON.**
