# 🆕 New Contributor Quick Start Guide

Welcome! This guide helps you add your first bug-free travel destination to our community repo.

## Step 1: Research Your Destination

Before adding, verify these key facts:
- [ ] Is it truly bug-free, or very low-bug? (be honest!)
- [ ] What are the best and worst months for insects?
- [ ] Why is it bug-free? (climate, altitude, wind, isolation?)
- [ ] Are there any exceptions or regional variations?
- [ ] Do you have sources? (travel blogs, tourism boards, pest reports)

## Step 2: Open a GitHub Issue

1. Go to the [Issues tab](https://github.com/zhub9006/bug-free-travel-guides/issues)
2. Click "New Issue"
3. Title: `New Destination: [Place Name]`
4. Fill out the template below

## Step 4: Share Your Experience

Even without formal research, your personal story counts! Just tell us:
- Where you went
- What bugs you saw (or didn't see!)
- What worked to keep bugs away
- What didn't work

## Tips for Success

1. **Be honest** — don't claim "zero bugs" unless you're sure
2. **Include sources** — helps verify and build trust
3. **Note exceptions** — "zero bugs EXCEPT in X area" is better than claiming perfection
4. **Mention seasonal changes** — bug conditions change year to year
5. **Add photos** — if you have bug-free sunset pics, share them! 📸

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I'm scared of bugs but want to visit a tropical destination. Is it possible?**
A: Yes! Many tropical destinations have very low bug pressure if you:
- Visit during the dry season
- Stay at resorts with pest control
- Stick to coastal/urban areas
- Use AC and screened rooms
- Avoid rainforests and standing water

**Q: What if I find bugs at my destination?**
A: That's valuable info! Report it — it helps others avoid the same issue. Include:
- When you went
- What bugs you encountered
- How many you saw
- Whether they bit you
- What you did to avoid them

**Q: Can I contribute without traveling?**
A: Absolutely! You can:
- Share destination research you've found online
- Fact-check existing guides
- Suggest improvements
- Share bug-free travel tips from your hometown
