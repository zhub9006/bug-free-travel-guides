## 🛡️ Top Pro Tips from Bug-Phobic Travelers

1. **Never skip permethrin-treated clothing** — this is non-negotiable
2. **Always carry DEET/Picaridin 20%+** — your safety net
3. **Stay on higher floors** — bugs travel upward, not down
4. **Use portable fans** — mosquitoes can't fly in strong wind
5. **Know your seasonal windows** — choose destinations at the right time
6. **Book resorts, not local guesthouses** — in tropical destinations
7. **Choose coastal/windy spots** — wind is nature's repellent
8. **Inspect hotels on arrival** — bed bugs are universal risk
9. **Sleep under nets if camping** — absolute protection
10. **Pack duplicate gear** — backup is always wise
