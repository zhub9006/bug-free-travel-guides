## 🏆 Destinations Ranked by Bug Safety

### ❄️ Antarctica — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Nov–Feb | Budget: $$–$$$$
- **The only 100% guarantee on Earth.** Extreme cold, no standing water, no vegetation. 100k+ visitors/year.

### 🏜️ Atacama Desert, Chile — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Mar–May/Sep–Nov | Budget: $$
- Driest desert on Earth (<1mm rainfall). No standing water. Stargazing capital.

### 🏝️ Faroe Islands — 0/10 ✅ | Season: May–Sep | Budget: $$–$$$
- Constant 30–50mph winds prevent insect flight. Bare granite, no wetlands.

### ✨ Hokkaido (Winter) — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Dec–Mar | Budget: $$–$$$
- Sub-zero kills all larvae. Snow festivals. Northern islands = best.

### 🏖️ Canary Islands (East — Lanzarote/Fuerteventura) — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Year-round | Budget: $$
- Trade winds + arid landscape. 300 days of sun. Make Lanzarote/Fuerteventura your pick.

### 🏜️ Dubai & Inland UAE — 1/10 | Season: Oct–Apr | Budget: $$–$$$$
- Hyper-arid, <100mm rainfall. Fogging systems. Avoid parks/Oasis areas.

### 🏜️ Phoenix/Sedona, AZ — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Mar–May/Sep–Nov | Budget: $$
- Sonoran Desert. Dry heat kills airborne insects. Fogging systems in Vegas.

### ❄️ Madeira, Portugal — 0.5/10 ⭐ | Season: Dec–Mar | Budget: $$
- Volcanic terrain + Atlantic winds. Stay on high ridges/coastal Funchal.

### ✨ Montserrat — 0.5/10 ⭐ | Season: Dec–Apr | Budget: $–$$$  |
- **Caribbean exception.** Volcanic soil drains water. **ONLY nearly-zero Caribbean destination.**

### ✨ Curaçao — 2/10 ⭐ | Season: Dec–Apr | Budget: $$–$$$  |
- Hyper-arid Caribbean island. NOT zero-bug but VERY low. DEET insurance.

### 🏝️ New Caledonia — 1.5/10 | Season: May–Oct | Budget: $$–$$$$
- Volcanic isolation + endemic plant repellents. Stick to west coast dry season.

### 🏝️ Azores, Portugal — 1–2/10 | Season: Jul–Sep | Budget: $$
- Windy, volcanic, oceanic. Low-density mosquitoes. Stay on high-altitude/coastal trails.

### 🏙️ UK/EU Cities — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Year-round | Budget: $$–$$$
- Cool rainy climate prevents mosquito breeding. EVERY major city. Perfect for severe phobia.

### 🏜️ Marrakech & Sahara, Morocco — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Mar–May/Sep–Nov | Budget: $–$$
- Arid rain-shadow. Morocco is MALARIA-FREE (CDC confirmed). Cedar Atlas optional.

### 🏝️ US Virgin Islands — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Dec–Apr | Budget: $$–$$$  |
- Ocean breezes + US government pest control. Stick to beachside hotels.

### 🏜️ Las Vegas, NV — 0/10 ✅ | Season: Mar–May/Sep–Nov | Budget: $$
- Mojave Desert + fogging systems. City is 100% bug-free. Avoid Red Rock canyons at dawn.

### 🏝️ Hawaii (Kona Coast, Big Island) — 2/10 ⚠️ | Season: Apr–Oct | Budget: $$–$$$
- **NOT zero-bug.** 2/10 with DEET + AC in dry season on western coast. **DO NOT visit Hilo, Volcano Village, or rainforest trails.** 8 mosquito species present.
