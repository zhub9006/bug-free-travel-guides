## ⚠️ Before You Read — Important Notes

- **"Bug-free" means minimal-to-zero biting insects (mosquitoes, sandflies, ticks, midges, etc.)**, not zero insects of any kind. Each destination is scored based on the density of *biting pests* that would ruin your trip.
- **Seasons are critically important** — even the most bug-free destinations have seasonal variations. Always check current conditions before booking.
- **A bug score of 1/10 = essentially bug-free**; **10/10 = severe bug infestation**. Pack DEET or picaridin as insurance even for low-score destinations.

## 💊 Universal Bug-Phobe Travel Kit (Essential for ALL trips)

Even the safest destinations benefit from preparedness. Here's what every bug-phobic traveler should pack:

| Item | Why | Recommended For |
|---|---|---|
| **DEET 30-50%** (Repel, Off, Sawyer) | Gold-standard mosquito/tick repellent | All tropical destinations, Egypt Nile, Scotland |
| **Picaridin 20-25%** (Cutter Advanced, Sawyer) | DEET-clinically shown alternative, more pleasant | Kona, Canaries, Costa Rica, Maldives |

| **Mosquito head net** | Physical barrier protection for sleep | Antarctica (luxury) only if extreme pre-caution |
| **Bug-free accommodation checklist** | Request rooms 50m from any standing water; confirm AC on (screen-sealed windows) | All destinations |
| **Light-colored clothing** | Mosquitoes are attracted to dark colors | All destinations |
| **Long-sleeve UV shirts** | Dual-purpose: sun protection + bug shield | All tropical destinations |
| **Portable fan** | Wind is a mosquito's enemy; fan creates >1mph breeze barrier | Hotels, tents, patio dining |

## 🤝 How to Contribute to This Guide

This is a **community-driven resource**. Here's how you can help:

### ✍️ Submit a New Destination

1. **Suggest** your destination by opening an issue with the label `destination-request`
2. **Research** using the [Submission Template](guides/submission-template.md) — you must include:
   - Verified bug score (1-10) based on personal experience + 2+ independent sources
   - Month-by-month seasonal safety calendar
   - Scientific explanation of WHY the place is bug-free
   - Budget tier and best-use case
   - Travel tips specific to bug-phobes
3. **Draft** your guide as a Markdown file in the `/guides/` directory
4. **Submit** a PR with your completed guide — flag it `needs-review`

### ✅ Review Guidelines

Every submitted destination must be:
- ✅ Cross-referenced with **at least 2 independent sources**
- ✅ Seasonal data verified within the last **12 months**
- ✅ Bug score justified with specific evidence
- ✅ Free of sponsored content or affiliate links
- ✅ Updated to reflect **current conditions** (climate change is shifting insect ranges fast!)
