# 🛡️ Essential Bug-Avoid Packing Checklist

Even on a "bug-free" trip, carry these for insurance against rare lapses.

---

| Item | Purpose | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| DEET 30–50% repellent OR Picaridin 20% | Primary defense | Apply to exposed skin every 4–6 hours |
| Permethrin-treated clothing (6+ wash durability) | Barrier protection | Treat shirts, pants, socks before travel |
| Mosquito net for accommodation | Traveler safety net | Even bug-free destinations can have lapses |
| Antihistamine cream | For unexpected bites | Reduces swelling and itching fast |
| Light-colored, long-sleeve convertible hiking pants | Physical barrier | Dark colors attract mosquitoes |
| Pre-treated gaiters for tick-heavy transitional zones | Tick protection | Essential for any grass/forest areas |
| Wasp/hornet spray for urban markets & restaurants | Stinging insect defense | Common in European outdoor restaurants |
| Sunscreen SPF 30+ + sun hat | Bug-free = often high UV exposure | Most bug-free destinations are sunny/dry |
| Lightweight head net | Worst-case unexpected swarms | Pack it; hope you never need it |

---

> **Philosophy: Pack one step beyond comfort. Even in Antarctica, rare intruders happen. Better safe than itchy!**
