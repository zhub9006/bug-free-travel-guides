## 🚨 Critical Corrections

### ⚠️ Seychelles is NOT Mosquito-Free
- **Reality:** Seychelles HAS mosquitoes year-round, especially Dec–Feb (rainy season)
- **Disease risk:** Malaria-free but dengue present; high mosquito density in wet season
- **Best safe window:** May–October with DEET as insurance
- **Correction date:** January 2026
- **Status:** ✅ Fixed in all references

### ⚠️ Iceland is No Longer 100% Mosquito-Free
- **Reality:** *Culiseta annulata* confirmed October 2025 in Kjós valley N of Reykjavík (2 females + 1 male)
- **Breeding status:** Unconfirmed at time of discovery; monitor Arctic Portal & Icelandic Nat. History before booking
- **Correction date:** February 2026
- **Impact:** Antarctica is now the ONLY guaranteed mosquito-free landmass
- **Status:** ✅ Fixed in all guides

### ⚠️ Patagonia: Winter ONLY
- **Reality:** November–March in Southern Hemisphere = aggressive sandflies; absolutely NOT for entomophobic travelers
- **Safe window:** ONLY June–August (their winter)
- **Correction date:** January 2026
- **Status:** ✅ Fixed in all guides
