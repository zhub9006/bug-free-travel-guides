## ✅ Submission Checklist

Before submitting, confirm:

- [ ] Bug score included with justification
- [ ] Month-by-month seasonal safety calendar provided
- [ ] Scientific explanation of WHY it's safe (not just "no bugs")
- [ ] Budget tier estimated
- [ ] At least 2 independent sources cited
- [ ] Key attractions and travel tips included

- [ ] No dangerous misinformation (verify against our critical corrections list in the README)

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Contributors

1. **Cross-reference everything** — if a source says "low mosquito," find a second source that confirms it
2. **Mention worst months too** — it's as important as best months for planning
3. **Include budget reality** — an amazing destination with a $10k/week price tag isn't useful for most bug-phobes
4. **Share local culture** — this isn't just a database; it's a community guide!
5. **Flag uncertainty** — if you're not 100% sure, note it in your submission and our reviewers will verify
