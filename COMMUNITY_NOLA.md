## 🗣️ Common Questions from New Members

### Q: Is it normal to be this afraid of bugs?
**A:** Absolutely! Your fear is completely valid. Many bug-phobic travelers report that visiting truly bug-free destinations helps reduce their anxiety over time. The key is to start with a zero-bug destination and build confidence from there.

### Q: What if I encounter a bug at my bug-free destination?
**A:** First of all, the odds are extremely low! But if it happens:
- Stay calm — most insects are harmless
- Use your repellent/fan as a precaution
- Request a room change if in a hotel
- Report it to the community so we can update our guides

### Q: Can I combine bug-free destinations on one trip?
**A:** Yes! Here are some popular combinations:
- **Iceland + Faroe Islands** — both Arctic, zero bugs
- **Atacama + Patagonia** — both dry/windy, minimal bugs
- **Maldives + Sri Lanka** — Indian Ocean circuit (avoid monsoon)
