# 📝 New Destination Guide Template

Use this template to create a new destination guide for the bug-free travel community!

## File Naming Convention

```
guides/destination-N-[destination-name].md
```

Example: `guides/destination-7-svalbard-norway.md`

## Required Sections

### 1. Header
```markdown
# 🌍 Destination Name — Bug-Free Travel Guide

**Bug Rating:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆ (X out of 5 — lower is better!)
```

### 2. Why It's Bug-Free
- **Explain the climate/geographic reasons** insects don't thrive
- **Cite specific factors** (temperature, humidity, altitude, water availability)
- **Keep it factual** — not speculative

### 3. Best Seasons to Visit (Table)
```markdown
| Season | Months | Bug Risk | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🏆 Best | MMM – MMM | Zero or Very Low | Weather + bug details |
| ✅ Good | MMM – MMM | Low | Any caveats |
| ⚠️ Avoid | MMM – MMM | Moderate–High | Why to avoid |
```

### 4. Rare Bug Notes (Important!)
- Any exceptions to the "bug-free" claim
- Where/when bugs *might* appear
- How to easily avoid them

### 5. Top Bug-Free Activities
List 4-6 activities that are completely bug-free

### 6. Tips for Bug-Phobic Travelers
- Specific prep tips for this destination
- What to pack (or what NOT to pack)
- Where to stay for maximum bug protection

### 7. Quick Stats
```markdown
- **Location:** City, Country + coordinates
- **Elevation:** X meters above sea level
- **Climate Type:** Mediterranean, desert, tundra, etc.
- **Average Humidity:** XX%
- **Nighttime Lows:** XX°C | **Daytime Highs:** XX°C
- **Nearest Large City:** [City]
- **Bug-Free Zone Percentage:** XX% of total destination
```

## Quality Standards

✅ **Must-haves:**
- At least 3 seasonal options with bug risk ratings
- Clear "why bug-free" explanation backed by climate data
- Honest assessment (don't overclaim — if there are ANY bugs, say so)
- At least 3 specific activities
- At least 3 practical tips

❌ **Don't:**
- Claim a destination is "100% bug-free" without proof
- Ignore exceptions or rare bug occurrences
- Use "probably" or "maybe" without noting they're unconfirmed
- Copy content without verifying it's current

## Review Process

1. Submit a PR with your new guide
2. Community reviewers check for accuracy and completeness
3. Maintainers verify seasonal data
4. Approved guides are merged and added to the README
