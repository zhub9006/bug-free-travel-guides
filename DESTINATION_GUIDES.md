| Destination | Score | Why to Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| Patagonia (Nov–Mar) | 10/10 | Sandfly hell — DO NOT VISIT in summer |
| Seychelles | 8/10 | Mosquitoes year-round, peak Dec–Feb |
| Bali, Indonesia | 9/10 | Extreme density; dengue everywhere |
| Belize | 8/10 | Highest mosquito density in Central America |
| Most of Africa, S.E. Asia, Amazon | 6–9/10 | High mosquito density; malaria/dengue risk |

## 📋 Seasonal Cheat Sheet

| Season | Best Destinations | Acceptable | Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Jan–Feb** | Antarctica, Faroe, Hokkaido, Atacama, Madeira, Montserrat ⭐, Curaçao ⭐, UK/EU, UAE/Oman | Hawaii, Dubai | Patagonia, Seychelles, S.E. Asia |
| **Mar–Apr** | Atacama, Madeira, Hokkaido, New Caledonia (shoulder), Curaçao, Sedona, UK/EU | Hawaii, Maldives | Seychelles, Patagonia |
| **May–Jun** | New Caledonia (dry), Faroe, UAE/Oman, Curaçao (start), Atacama | Hawaii, Maldives | Patagonia, S.E. Asia, French Polynesia |
| **Jul–Aug** | Faroe, Madeira, New Caledonia, Maldives resorts, Kona (best), UK/EU, Hokkaido | UAE/Oman, Sedona | Patagonia, Africa, S.E. Asia |
| **Sep–Oct** | New Caledonia, Atacama, UAE/Oman, Montserrat ⭐, Curaçao ⭐ | UK/EU Cities | Seychelles wet start, French Polynesia late wet |
| **Nov–Dec** | UAE/Oman, Madeira, Montserrat ⭐, Curaçao ⭐, UK/EU Cities | Hawaii (spotty) | French Polynesia wet, Seychelles peak |
| **Dec–Apr** | **BEST CARIBBEAN:** Montserrat ⭐ & Curaçao ⭐ | Maldives resorts | Seychelles, S.E. Asia |

> **⚠️ Seasonal Traps:** Patagonia = June–Aug ONLY (Nov–Mar = sandfly hell!), Hawaii peaks Sept–Nov (Apr–Oct best)
