# Bug-Free Travel Guides

A community hub for bug-phobic travelers to share safe, insect-free travel destinations, seasonal tips, and practical guides.

## What Is This?

If you are terrified of bugs - mosquitoes, ticks, flies, spiders - this repo is your safe haven. We compile the world's most bug-free destinations, identify the safest seasons to visit each one, and build a growing community of travelers who refuse to let insects ruin their adventures.

## Tips for Bug-Phobic Travelers

- Choose windy and coastal destinations - wind is nature's best repellent
- Visit in winter or shoulder seasons - cold kills insect activity
- Prefer islands - isolation prevents insect colonization
- Book accommodations with screens/AC
- Pack DEET as a precaution
- Avoid tropical rainforests and stagnant water

## Destinations We Are Looking For
