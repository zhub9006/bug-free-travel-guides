## 🛡️ Universal Bug-Phobic Travel Tips

| Tip | Why It Works |
|-----|-------------|
| Permethrin-treated clothing | Kills insects on contact |
| DEET/Picaridin repellent (20%+) | Proven mosquito/disease prevention |
| Wear light-colored clothing | Mosquitoes are attracted to dark colors |
| Avoid perfumes/scented lotions | Scent attracts mosquitoes |
| Stay in AC-equipped accommodations | Cool + sealed = no bugs |
| Choose dry-season travel | Less rain = less standing water = fewer bugs |
| Check CDC travel advisories | Disease & bug risk updates by country |
| Pack a small mosquito net | Peace of mind for bug-sensitive sleepers |
| Learn about local sandflies | NZ & Scotland have sandflies (not mosquitoes!) |
| Research seasonal peaks | Most bugs are seasonal — timing is everything |
